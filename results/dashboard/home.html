About This Data

This platform analyzes the Anthropic Economic Index (AEI), which measures AI task competition patterns across occupations. The data captures how many job incumbents respond to various AI-related tasks, revealing inequality in how competition distributes across the labor market.

Key dimensions include occupation titles, SOC (Standard Occupational Classification) major groups, task types (Core vs. Supplemental), and temporal trends from 2003-2015.

Data source: Hugging Face - Anthropic Economic Index
